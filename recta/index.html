About

The essence of life is to always be connected to the right People and Places. Find people you love. Find places you love. Start connecting and start living life to the fullest.

We understand that the student experience is a wonderful one, but not complete without the right people and place. That is why we have come up with this solution to help YOU get that complete student experience.

One Vision

We have just one Vision - to connect students to the right people and places.

Two Apps

We connect students in two phases through two apps. First to people they love through the Frend App and then to places they love through the Recta App.

Just For You

Both apps were designed with You in mind. To best meet your desire to connect to People and Places.

Connect with People You Love using Frend.

Are you tired of having shallow relationships? Or tired of having that feeling of loneliness? Or tired of having friends that don't understand your personality. Start finding people who are like you and connect to them with Frend.

Create an account. Complete your tailored profile. Find people who are just like you. And take advantage of Frend's design to help you connect intimately with people you love.

It is a new way of connecting to people; a better way to LOVE.

Connect to Places You Love using Recta.

Have you ever faced the dilemma of wanting to go out but not knowing exactly where to go? Have you ever wanted the best but don't which place was best? Have you wanted to go somewhere new but didn't know how to find new places? Never face such experience again, use Recta to find places you love.

  • Search for places you want to go.
  • Find new places in a category.
  • See places and get descriptions to help you decide.
  • Get directions to the places you want to visit.
  • Get news and events happening in places you love.

It is a new way of connecting to the places you love. Don't be bored. Don't go to the wrong places. Use Recta.
